My ideologies

Make a plan

A plan gives you concrete steps to follow. It allows you to allot the proper amount of time for the task. Even if you don't follow the plan, it's there to guide you if need be.

The business of people

We will always need the power of people and teams of people. Working together exponentially increases productivity, and working with people you like is one of the keys to happiness.

Learn to teach

What good is a genius if they are not understandable? Being able to communicate your thoughts clearly is a skill and takes practice.

Know your constraints

If I had unlimited time and resources, I could make anything perfect. But that is not possible due to constraints. Focus on the priorities that are most important.

Be a small fish

You want to be a small fish in a big pond. The best way to grow is to surround yourself with people who are smarter than you.
